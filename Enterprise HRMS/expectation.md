Buikd an Enterprise Human Resources Management System using the requiment listed below and always refer to it to confirm the list of feature expected in it.
HRMS Features
1. Core HR & Employee Data Management
Employee records (demographics, gender [configurable to Male/Female])

Organizational charts

Document management (contracts, certifications)

Custom fields for compliance

2. Recruitment & Applicant Tracking (ATS)
Job posting & career portals

Resume parsing & AI-based candidate matching

Interview scheduling & feedback

Onboarding workflows

3. Time & Attendance
Clock-in/out (biometric, mobile, web)

Shift scheduling

Overtime & leave tracking

Geolocation tracking (for remote/field workers)

4. Leave & Absence Management
Policy configuration (gender-neutral or binary, if required)

Leave requests & approvals

Sick leave, vacation, maternity/paternity leave

Compliance with local labor laws

5. Payroll & Compensation
Automated payroll processing

Tax compliance (local & international)

Bonuses, incentives, and deductions

Gender-based reporting (if legally required)

6. Performance Management
Goal setting (OKRs/KPIs)

360-degree feedback

Performance reviews & calibration

Succession planning

7. Learning & Development (LMS)
Course management & certifications

AI-driven skill recommendations

Compliance training

Integration with LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, etc.

8. Employee Self-Service (ESS) & Portals
Personal data updates (within legal gender constraints)

Pay slips & tax documents

Benefits enrollment

9. Benefits Administration
Health insurance, retirement plans

Flexible benefits (gender-based if applicable)

COBRA & compliance reporting

10. Workforce Analytics & Reporting
Custom dashboards

Headcount, turnover, diversity (as per legal gender recognition)

Predictive analytics for attrition

11. Compliance & Security
GDPR/local data privacy compliance

Audit trails & role-based access

Electronic signatures

12. Mobile HR & AI Assistants
Chatbots for HR queries

Mobile app for managers & employees

How These Features Interoperate in an HR Ecosystem
Function	Integration Points
Recruitment → Onboarding	ATS feeds new hire data into Core HR & triggers onboarding tasks (e.g., document signing).
Time Tracking → Payroll	Attendance records auto-sync with payroll for accurate salary processing.
Performance → Learning	Skill gaps identified in reviews trigger personalized LMS courses.
Leave → Payroll	Approved leave days deduct automatically from payroll calculations.
Core HR → Analytics	Employee data fuels workforce reports (e.g., gender-based headcount for compliance).
ESS → Benefits	Employees self-select benefits via the portal, updating payroll deductions.
System Interoperability
Most modern HRMS platforms integrate via:

APIs (REST/SOAP) – e.g., Workday ↔ ADP for payroll.

Middleware (MuleSoft, Dell Boomi) – Connects SAP SuccessFactors to legacy systems.

Single Sign-On (SSO) – Employees use one login for HR, email, and other tools.

Cloud Sync – Oracle HCM ↔ BambooHR for SMEs needing hybrid systems.

Best All-in-One Systems for Gender-Binary Compliance
SAP SuccessFactors (Best for global enterprises needing deep customization).

Workday (Seamless integration across HR + finance).

Oracle HCM (Strong compliance + AI-driven analytics).

BambooHR (Best for SMEs needing simplicity + binary gender fields).
