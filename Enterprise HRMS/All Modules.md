1. Core HR (HRIS)
Purpose: Centralize employee data and organizational structure.
Key Features:

Employee records (bio, gender [configurable], job history)

Org charts & reporting hierarchies

Custom workflows (promotions, transfers)

Document management (contracts, policies)

Compliance tracking (labor laws, data privacy)

2. Global Talent Acquisition (ATS)
Purpose: Streamline hiring across regions.
Key Features:

AI-powered candidate sourcing (LinkedIn, Indeed)

Automated interview scheduling (Zoom/Teams integration)

Employer branding portals

Offer letter generation & e-signatures (DocuSign)

Onboarding task automation (IT access, training)

3. Workforce Planning & Analytics
Purpose: Optimize headcount and costs.
Key Features:

Headcount forecasting (AI-driven models)

Skills gap analysis

Scenario modeling (mergers, layoffs)

Diversity metrics (gender, ethnicity per legal requirements)

4. Time & Attendance
Purpose: Track productivity and compliance.
Key Features:

Biometric/facial recognition clocks

Real-time geofencing for remote workers

Fatigue management alerts

Labor law-compliant break tracking

5. Payroll & Compensation
Purpose: Ensure accurate, compliant payments.
Key Features:

Multi-country payroll (auto-tax calculations)

Executive compensation (stock options, bonuses)

Pay equity analysis (gender/role comparisons)

Garnishment management

6. Benefits Administration
Purpose: Manage global benefits efficiently.
Key Features:

Health insurance enrollment (US: ACA, EU: EHIC)

Pension/401(k) auto-enrollment

Flexible spending accounts (FSAs)

Global mobility benefits (expatriate housing)

7. Performance Management
Purpose: Drive employee growth and alignment.
Key Features:

Continuous feedback tools (real-time pulse surveys)

9-box grid for talent segmentation

Calibration tools to reduce bias

Career pathing visualizations

8. Learning & Development (LMS)
Purpose: Upskill at scale.
Key Features:

AI-curated learning paths (based on role/skills)

Certification tracking (automated expiry alerts)

Virtual instructor-led training (Zoom/MS Teams)

Leadership pipeline programs

9. Employee Experience
Purpose: Boost engagement and retention.
Key Features:

Recognition platforms (peer bonuses, badges)

Exit interview analytics

AI chatbot for HR queries ("What’s my PTO balance?")

Workplace collaboration tools (Slack/Teams integration)

10. Compliance & Risk Management
Purpose: Mitigate legal and security risks.
Key Features:

Automated audit trails (SOX, GDPR)

Whistleblower case management

Policy attestation tracking

Background check integration (Sterling, Checkr)

11. Mobile & Self-Service
Purpose: Empower employees and managers.
Key Features:

Mobile app for approvals (leave, expenses)

Digital payslips and tax forms

Push notifications for deadlines

Voice-enabled assistants ("Approve PTO via Alexa")

12. Integration Hub
Purpose: Connect HRMS with other enterprise systems.
Key Features:

Pre-built ERP connectors (SAP, Oracle)

API marketplace (Workday, Salesforce)

Data sync with IT (Active Directory, Okta)

Custom workflow builders (low-code tools)
